This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Bangalore, Karnataka.

No amendment to this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both parties.
